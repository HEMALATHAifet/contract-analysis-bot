EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into on 15/02/2025 between
Bright Future Solutions Pvt Ltd and the Employee.

1. Appointment
The Employee shall be appointed as Software Developer for a period
of 2 years.

2. Confidentiality
The Employee shall not disclose any confidential information during
or after the term of employment.

3. Termination
The Employer may terminate the employment without cause by giving
15 days notice.

4. Non-Compete
The Employee shall not work with any competing organization for a
period of 2 years after termination.

5. Jurisdiction
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of India and subject to
the jurisdiction of Bengaluru courts.
